About SMITW

Sri Mittapalli Institute Of Technology For Women (SMITW) is a premier engineering college located in Thummalapalem, Guntur District, Andhra Pradesh. Established in 2008.

Our campus spreads over 5 acres with modern laboratories, digital classrooms, a central library with over 50,000 volumes, and excellent sports facilities.
